About me

I currently work as a research fellow at Institute for Financial Studies, Shandong University. My research interest includes high-dimensional statistical inference, financial econometrics, biostatistics and statistical learning. I received my PhD from the Fudan University in 2017 (advised by Xinsheng Zhang) and my Bachelor's degree in mathematics from Shandong University in 2012.

Education

Ph.D, Fudan University, 06.2017

B.Sc, Shandong University, 06.2012

Research

My research interests include:

  • Financial Econometrics: Large-dimensional factor model; Quantile analysis; Risk Management; Portfolio Allocation.

  • High-dimensional Statistical Inference: Variable Selection; Graphical Model; Large-scale multiple testing; Copula; Tensor data.

  • Biostatistics: Multi-omics data; functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging data.

  • Machine (Statistical) Learning: Supervised/Unsupervised Learning; Regularization Method; Distributed computation; Optimization problems; Online updating/detection.
