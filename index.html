About me

I currently work as a professor at Institute for Financial Studies, Shandong University. My research interest includes financial econometrics, high-dimensional statistical inference, statistical machine learning and biostatistics. I received my PhD from the Fudan University in 2017 (advised by Xinsheng Zhang) and my Bachelor's degree in mathematics from Shandong University in 2012.

Research Interest

My research interests include:

  • Financial Econometrics: Factor model; Quantile analysis; Risk Management; Portfolio Allocation; Empirical Finance.

  • High-dimensional Statistical Inference: Matrix-valued data and High-order Tensor data; Dimension Reduction (PCA); Graphical Model; Large-scale multiple testing.

  • Machine (Statistical) Learning: Transfer Learning; Federated Learning; Supervised/Unsupervised Learning (Clustering); Regularization Method; Distributed Statistical Inference; Online updating/detection.

  • Biostatistics: Multi-omics data; functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging data (fMRI).
